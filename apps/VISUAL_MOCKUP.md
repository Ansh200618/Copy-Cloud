## Typography

- **Font Family**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headers**: Bold, 20-28sp
- **Body**: Regular, 14-16sp
- **Buttons**: Medium, 14-16sp
- **Code Display**: Mono, Bold, 32sp

## Dimensions

- **Screen Width**: Responsive (320dp - 600dp+)
- **Min Height**: 640dp
- **Padding**: 16dp standard
- **Button Height**: 48dp
- **Input Height**: 56dp minimum
- **Icon Size**: 24dp standard

## Animations

- **Splash Duration**: 2000ms
- **Page Transitions**: 300ms ease
- **Button Presses**: 150ms scale
- **Toast Notifications**: 3000ms display
