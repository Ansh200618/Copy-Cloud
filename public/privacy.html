Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Here is how we handle your data when you use Copy Cloud:

Data Handling & Encryption

Files and text are stored securely in our database and are only accessible via the unique 6-character code generated at the time of upload.

  • Data Deletion: All user data is permanently purged from our systems automatically after the 24-hour expiration period. We do not keep backups of expired clips.
  • Anonymity: We do not require account creation, email addresses, or personal information to use this tool. You remain completely anonymous.
  • Cookies & Local Storage: We use minimal local storage for functional purposes only, such as saving your theme preference (dark/light mode) and keeping a local log of your recent activity history. We do not use tracking cookies for targeted advertising.
