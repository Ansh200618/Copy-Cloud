Copy Cloud — The Free Online Clipboard for Instant, Anonymous Transfers

            
Copy Cloud is your go-to online clipboard for fast, secure, and no-login file and text transfer. Whether you need to copy paste online between your phone and computer, share text between devices, or use temporary file storage that auto-deletes after 24 hours, Copy Cloud has you covered.

            
Unlike traditional cloud storage services, Copy Cloud is designed as a secure clipboard that prioritizes simplicity and privacy. There is no account creation, no email required, and no tracking — making it the best choice for anonymous file transfer. Simply upload your text or file, receive a unique 6-character code, and retrieve it from any device. It is truly a cross-platform clipboard that works seamlessly across Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android.

            
Looking for a way to transfer files without an account? Need to quickly copy text to your phone from your desktop or paste from your computer on the go? Copy Cloud is the free file sharing tool built exactly for that. Our cloud clipboard ensures your data is available when you need it and gone when you don't — every clip expires automatically after 24 hours for your security and peace of mind.
