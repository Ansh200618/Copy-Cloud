Stego Tool

Hide encrypted messages inside ordinary text using invisible zero-width characters. End-to-end encrypted, 100% client-side.

This text will be visible — the secret is hidden after it.

Click copy — the hidden data is embedded after the visible text.

How It Works

1

Your message is encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a key derived from your password via PBKDF2 (100,000 iterations).

2

The encrypted bytes are converted to invisible zero-width characters (U+200B and U+200C) and appended after the cover text.

3

The result looks like ordinary text but carries a hidden payload. Only someone with the correct password can recover the message.

4

Everything happens in your browser — no data is ever sent to a server.
