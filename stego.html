How It Works

1

Text → Text: Your message (optionally encrypted with AES-256-GCM) is converted to invisible zero-width characters and appended after cover text.

2

File → Text: Images or documents are encoded into zero-width characters with metadata (filename, type). The result is copyable text.

3

Text → Image: Your message is hidden in a cover image using LSB steganography — changing the least significant bit of pixel colors, invisible to the eye.

4

Everything happens in your browser — no data is ever sent to a server. Passwords use PBKDF2 (100k iterations).
